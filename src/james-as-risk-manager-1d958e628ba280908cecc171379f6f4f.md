# Overview

James official job title is Risk Manager.

He works in the field of Risk Mitigation.

The intent is for irony: his job should sound dull and unneeded, when it is actually dangerous and critical.

## Background

For many years I struggled to find a good work role for James.

Part of the blindside is I’ve been looking for an ordinary role for James, but I kept defaulting to blue collar positions.  I wasn’t thinking in terms of white color positions.

It achieves the desired affect of underappreciation I was looking for, but in a different way.

Instead of looking lowly, the role looks stuffy.

On paper, his job is problem prevention.  In practice, people don’t listen to him and his job is problem fixing.  His involvement follows the following flow:

1. Research the situation.
2. Submit recommendations.
3. Prepare for everything to go wrong.
4. Fix things as much as possible when everything goes wrong.

In some respects, his involvement is similar to Jeeves in  [Jeeves and Wooster](jeeves-and-wooster-18658e628ba280b781bfcabd1d05cd5d.md).
