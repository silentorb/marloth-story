# Maximize isolation and society


As much as possible, each scene should either contain [Isolation](isolation-330cda5056f64459b8d0d46a66ed21a2.md) or warm social tones such as [Friends and family dining together](friends-and-family-dining-together-841883629b3544f182d9ec275e8afecd.md).

The rules of thumb are as follows:

1. If a scene has two characters:
    1. And the tone and dynamics would be marred if more characters were added, leave the scene alone.
    2. Otherwise, add more characters.
2. If a scene primarily has a single character, or a single character and an adversary, make it feel like there is no one else in the world, and double down on introspection.

<aside>
💡 Isolation and social warmth should act as gravitational pulls, pulling each scene toward whichever pole the scene is closer to.

</aside>
