# Standing for righteousness

It’s not enough to be good—good characters need to be backed into situations where they need to take a stand.

This is especially important for [James](james-14f6a0f77a694f4cb18881bb58846c94.md), who is passive until he isn’t.  It is pressure that causes him to come alive.
