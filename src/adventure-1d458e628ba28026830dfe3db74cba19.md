# Wonderland

This page is tagged as an aspect of [Wonderland](wonderland-3cbc40d2ba2a4c76b4b9dc370452fcfe.md).

While the original Wonderland books didn’t exactly have the modern sense of adventure, they did have *a* sense of adventure.

After all, the first book was named “Alice’s *Adventures* in Wonderland”.
