# Assessment

I don’t have fond memories of this show, and I stopped watching near the latter few seasons.

However, *The Walking Dead* is distinctive and thought-provoking.

It’s the most sophisticated zombie narrative I’ve ever seen.

# Moral drama

One of the most engaging aspects of *The Walking Dead* is how much it explores morality.

I don’t agree with its moral conclusions, and I’m not talking about individual character’s decisions.

I’m meaning, they don’t accurately depict the way the world works.  It’s speculative fiction and the speculation is based on faulty premises.

# Survival horror

As an extended narrative, Walking Dead also has the opportunity to present a rich survival scenario.
