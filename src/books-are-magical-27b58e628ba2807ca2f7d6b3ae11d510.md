# Books are magical

[Products: TWOLD](twold-e028aa0786f5449984a4f497c1d746fa.md)[, A Child’s Fairytale World](a-childs-fairytale-world-c368a02eabb74a47ab322615f2769723.md)

This was a mechanic in [A Child’s Fairytale World](a-childs-fairytale-world-c368a02eabb74a47ab322615f2769723.md), and it may be worth including in more of the narrative.

# Problem

This is an extreme feature which makes high demands of its surroundings and can quickly create plot conflicts.

<aside>
💡

It sort of worked in [A Child’s Fairytale World](a-childs-fairytale-world-c368a02eabb74a47ab322615f2769723.md) due to there being hardly any books in the world outside of [The Great Library](the-great-library-27e58e628ba280b0948bd833ae53039b.md).

</aside>

In order to continue implementing this idea, it would need one or more limiting mechanics to make its effects manageable.

# Limiting effect

## Only certain books

One limit would be only certain books or types of books are magical.
