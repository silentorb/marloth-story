A magic user inspired by Gandalf and the black mage from Final Fantasy I.

A character from the books I wrote during my childhood and teens: “Legacies Realm”, “Heroes Realm”, and “Oak”.

In earlier literature he was one of the heroes, but now he seems better as a more neutral party.  He is not evil, but he is also a maverick that has his own agenda.
