# No clever outwitting


While I enjoy battles of the wits, Marloth is a moral drama.  Marloth should be composed of [Ideological contests](../../Features/Ideological%20contests%203095477179ab4fd1a6977f151ff2aef8.md).

Heroes should not win due to their cleverness, but by the hand of the Lord and virtue.

[Rely on Jesus, not human wisdom](rely-on-jesus-not-human-wisdom-d2184c188bd44b989a579bea0fc92e4b.md) 

<aside>
💡 If anything, relying on cleverness should get characters into trouble.

</aside>
