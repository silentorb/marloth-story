# Drenodir’s loyalty to the Medievs conflicts with interweaving Nivana with Adelle storylines

Adelle is Nivana’s friend.  It makes sense for her and James to rescue Nivana.

However, if Drenodir sees himself as a guardian of the Medievs, it would be expected for him to rescue Nivana.

<aside>
💡 It is also harder to explain the delay before Drenodir helps Nivana.  I can make excuses but they all feel contrived.  With James and Adelle, the delay feels more natural.

</aside>

I wouldn’t even mind if Drenodir was the one who rescued Nivana, as long as Adelle instigated it.  If there is any outreach toward Nivana, I want Adelle to be the driving force.
