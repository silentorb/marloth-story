# Desa arc

A series of interesting events that happen with [Desa](../../Data/Characters/Desa%2021dde76cb7b947fd896ecf352acd6929.md).

I don’t really have many ideas for this yet.  Desa is a side character but his involvement is getting just elaborate enough to warrant an arc, but I don’t know what the themes or conclusions would be yet.

> 💡 The best next step in such cases is to compare his character to the current values, foils, and requirements of the story.

# Magic

If nothing else, Desa’s involvement in TWOLD revolves around magic.  Not only is Desa a wizard, but he is also a magical being.  He is inherently magical and needs magic energy to survive.

# Rogue wizard

Desa is a wizard, but he isn’t a member of the wizards’ council, which makes him a rogue wizard.  That’s an interesting angle.

> 💡 Maybe Desa used to be a member of the wizard’s guild?

> 💡 Probably Desa is hunted by the wizards.  Not directly, of course.  They would hire some type of agents to pursue him.

> 💡 Oh, that’s right, Desa starts off in prison.  All these years (all the way back to my teens) and I never really came up with a backstory as to who imprisoned him and why.
> Now I have a good context.  Desa is imprisoned by the wizards’ council for leaving, or some other action the see as betrayal.
