# James submitted to Tralvorkemen

[Products: TWOLD](twold-e028aa0786f5449984a4f497c1d746fa.md)

I’ve been throwing around the idea of having James being a subcontractor for [The Agency](../Features/The%20Agency%2044db7ec455e14b10a9bc1da088f20f66.md), with [Edward Tralvorkemen](edward-tralvorkemen-27b58e628ba2802cac4fdedacf73cf7d.md) being his actual employer.

I like the idea of this being an implicit background thread, without Tralvorkemen ever explicitly mentioned.

For most of the story, the audience can settle into a sense that James directly works for the Agency, with occasional hints briefly disrupting that sense.

[The Bear](the-bear-95e68e70d28b409281fa2b931537bb79.md) is aware of the business relationship and occasionally refers to James’ employer.

[The Bear](the-bear-95e68e70d28b409281fa2b931537bb79.md) has a healthy fear and deference to [Edward Tralvorkemen](edward-tralvorkemen-27b58e628ba2802cac4fdedacf73cf7d.md).

# Stewardship
