# Grand climax

# Ongoing adventure

I have no idea what this will be yet.

Unlike the [A Child’s Fairytale World](a-childs-fairytale-world-c368a02eabb74a47ab322615f2769723.md), I haven’t been designing this with an end-game in mind.

Part of the reason is [TWOLD](twold-e028aa0786f5449984a4f497c1d746fa.md) is an exercise in moving away from discrete stories and to create a [Long-running series](long-running-series-a42ad48c6e9842909f53755c3884a54a.md).

At least for now, I’m tired of writing stories that revolve around their ending.
