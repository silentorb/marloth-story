# Non-goals

I don’t want it to be about being a millennial man-child or playboy that can’t settle down.  I want it to be timeless.

I don’t want the tension to appear 50:50.  Far more people are maintainers than pioneers.  A little innovation goes a long way.

# Bible

What are the Biblical precedents?

Jesus had no home.  Though He was also single.  The church is His bride.

Most of the people and circumstances in the Bible are static, with the exception of:

1. Abraham, Isaac, and Jacob
2. The Exodus
3. Certain prophets
4. The Exiles
5. Missionaries

Even when people in the Bible are wandering, they are still usually heading to a promised land.

# Symbolism

What are symbols for static vs dynamic?

## Static symbols

Sturdy, stationary objects are static.  This includes:

1. Trees
2. Rocks
3. Mountains
4. Buildings
5. Home

## Dynamic symbols

Mobile, mercurial objects are dynamic.  This includes:

1. Wind
2. Streams
3. Weather
4. Seasons
5. Vehicles
6. Birds
