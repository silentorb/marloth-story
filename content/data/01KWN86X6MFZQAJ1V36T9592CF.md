# Parable character

A character who is used as an object lesson involving moral decisions.

Parable characters are usually tragic and demonstrate what a person *shouldn’t* do.

> 💡 Parable characters are simpler than [[01KWN86X6MFZQAJ1V36T9592CG]]s, while their souls and motivations are explored more than [[01KWN86X6MFZQAJ1V36T9592CE]]s.
