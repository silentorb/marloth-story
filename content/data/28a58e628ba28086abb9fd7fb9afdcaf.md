# Overview

> 💡This is a broader sense of [[e54012bfd6544f37968a4e9899f53ca0]].

Wilderness settings provides the bulk of the horror in [[e5cc80dc61ed4c629951cdf472b20b7a]].

The wilderness is dangerous and scary.  When characters are in the wilderness they can never let their guard down.

For Marloth stories, the horror of Cozy horror should largely be [[dc101b6438cf43a8b5f1e17212b8c950]].

# Sanctuaries

The antithesis of wilderness is [[28a58e628ba280bdb83effe9f4031ff5]].

Wilderness settings should usually be cold and dark, in contrast with the warm and light of [[28a58e628ba280bdb83effe9f4031ff5]].

# Ingredients

The ingredients of a cozy horror wilderness setting are:

1. Travel
2. Industry
3. Dangers
4. Isolation

# Industry

Sometimes characters must perform activities within the wilderness.

In those cases, the character isn’t so much traveling between sanctuaries as traveling to a site, working there, then returning to a sanctuary.

> 💡 The longer the duration of the work activity, the greater the danger.

# Dangers

The cozy horror wilderness dangers are a subset of [[28458e628ba28099bf9dde1a919592c5]].

That is because most cozy horror dangers are [[7cb1f51a24da45e3b29290fc063ae3ed]] dangers.

> 💡For example, hunger is *not* a major concern when traveling in the wilderness.
>
> When spending several days at a time in the wilderness, hunger can be a greater concern, but so are all of the other dangers.

# Isolation

Wilderness does not always have [[330cda5056f64459b8d0d46a66ed21a2]], but isolation usually takes place in the wilderness.
