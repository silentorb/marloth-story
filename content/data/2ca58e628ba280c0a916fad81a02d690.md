# The Shadow Empire

A mysterious, otherworldly dimension, hostile to the primary dimension(s).

Sees the primary dimension(s) as rebel colonies that must be returned to its rule.

Initially mentioned in [[79bb690186294ddfbd417bfcc108b5ac]].

Usually only brought up in city settings.
