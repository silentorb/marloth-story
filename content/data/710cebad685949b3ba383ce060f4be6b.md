# Millamer trying to get ahead

# Underdog hustler

One of the most interesting roles I keep coming back to with Millamer is him being an enterprising individual that is always getting the short end of the stick. 

Someone who is tactical, resourceful, and adaptable.

And a little unscrupulous.

# Non-magic

Regardless, for a long time I’ve liked the idea of Millamer being at a disadvantage, being a non-magical character in a magical world.

Despite his disadvantage, through resourcefulness, good instincts, and determination, he is able to survive and thrive.
