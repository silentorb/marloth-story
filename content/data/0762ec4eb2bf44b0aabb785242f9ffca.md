# Scandalous Mediev backstory

An idea I’ve had for a long time is for a backstory where [[a2ee9cd0a28b4945af74fd3022fd97af]]’s father and uncle created a machine to extract magic from women and one of the first women they tried it on was Nivana’s mother.  It drove her partially insane.  The [[1b19d49697be4210828da0f74c16f8e9]] had a change of heart and reformed, turning his back on his brother and eventually marrying the woman.

The rest of his life has been an attempted atonement for his past.

Nivana discovers this history near the end of TWOLD.

> 💡 After all these years, this is the first time I’ve written the idea down, and at first I was afraid doing so and casting light on it would reveal it to be silly or over-the-top, but I think the opposite is the case.  This story feels both classic and distinct.
