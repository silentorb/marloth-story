# Overview

I want to encourage the audience to value [[020cdb46039646e69221612be7a7cf4a]], but not just any kind of marriage, marriage built on [[bdacc489959e4e39b8e3a86c7dede268]] commitment.

# Primary depiction

How do I depict this?

Living life together as a team.

# Ingredients

1. A husband [[14f6a0f77a694f4cb18881bb58846c94]]
2. A wife [[2ff1980fd10b42a7bc0f65b61a2ab3e8]]
3. A home [[21758e628ba280efbd31c44c44092e48]]
4. The truth 

<br />

# Specific ideas

1. [[40648921533242b3b7695a3f120931ee]]
2. Confiding in each other
3. Encouraging each other when they are down

   1. Making each other smile/laugh
   2. Always being there when the other needs them
4. Complimentary roles, strengths, and weaknesses

   1. Serving others as a team
5. Serving each other
6. Correcting/exhorting each other

   1. Pointing each other to Jesus
   2. Washing his wife in the Word
   3. Submitting to her spiritual head
7. Being patient with each other
8. Forgiving each other
9. Ambient companionship
