# Curses

This category covers three forms of curses:

1. Living humans who are cursed (preferably due to their own wickedness or mistakes) which turn them into monsters and are antagonists (I like to keep this separate from undead, who can also be reanimated by a curse.)
2. Broad curses that are effectively obstacles for the heroes to overcome.
3. Curses on the heroes which are thorns in their sides.

I could split those into separate entries, except that on average, if a narrative has one of them, it has all three.
