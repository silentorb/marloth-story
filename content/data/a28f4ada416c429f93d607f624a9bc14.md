# James and Adelle meet

“Adelle!”

Adelle turned around.

“James!”

“I’m so sorry I’m late!”

“Are you married?”

“No!  I am quite single.”

“This is just like a dream!”

“Yes, but who’s dream is it?”

Adelle’s eyes glistened.

“It’s both of ours.”
