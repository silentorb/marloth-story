# Magic mind screw

The idea that magic messes with reality and messes with magic user’s sense of reality, similar to being a [Travelers](./bbc494a7892947fd8bc516fab446f6da.md).

The main difference is Travelers are better at adapting to the overlap and becoming one with it, where magic users must carefully ration it more.

I’m thinking of having [Nivana](./a2ee9cd0a28b4945af74fd3022fd97af.md) experience a heavy dose of this.
