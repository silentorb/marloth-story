# Personal

While I am an extremely abstract, cerebral person, there is still an aspect of me that continually yearns for tangible depth.

I am acutely sensitive to cases where art lacks that depth.

Such cases feel hollow to me, like someone who started painting a picture and never finished, leaving a mostly blank canvas.
