# James is pressed into a deadly game

James began unbuttoning his shirt.

James took out a piece of paper and jotted a note.

James took off his coat.

James took off his shirt.

James took up one of the pistols, pointed it at his heart, and discharged the weapon.

The note said, “Read the book aloud.”
