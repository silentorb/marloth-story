# Books are magical

This was a mechanic in [[c368a02eabb74a47ab322615f2769723]], and it may be worth including in more of the narrative.

# Problem

This is an extreme feature which makes high demands of its surroundings and can quickly create plot conflicts.

> 💡 It sort of worked in [[c368a02eabb74a47ab322615f2769723]] due to there being hardly any books in the world outside of [[27e58e628ba280b0948bd833ae53039b]].

In order to continue implementing this idea, it would need one or more limiting mechanics to make its effects manageable.

# Limiting effect

## Only certain books

One limit would be only certain books or types of books are magical.
