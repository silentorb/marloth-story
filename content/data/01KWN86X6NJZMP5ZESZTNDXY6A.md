# Hidden monsters living amongst us

> 💡 Closely related to [[01KWN86X6MFZQAJ1V36T9592DY]] , but they can exist independently.
> This feature focuses on the tension of sharing space with fantastic dangers.  The denizens may or may not be aware of the monsters.

> 💡 The “hidden” in the title is not to say that the monsters are completely invisible, but are hiding or hard to see.  People may not need special powers to notice the monsters.
> The main point is that they aren’t walking around in plain sight.
> Seeing one is always a shock.
