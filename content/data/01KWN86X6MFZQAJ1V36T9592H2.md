# Extended survival horror

Extended survival horror is a sub-genre of [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXY7P]].

> 💡 This sub-genre is in partial contrast to ‣.

Extended survival horror has all of the requirements of [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXY7P]] plus the following additions:

# Long duration

Must involve a significantly long enough time period to require sustenance and not just avoiding dangers

> 💡 That is what ‣ as a genre normally means.
> Robinson Crusoe is a classic survival story.

This requirement rules out many horror movies where characters are required to survive some monster over the course of a single night.
