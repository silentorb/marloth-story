# Articulate domains by professions

# Problem

I’ve had a difficult time articulating various domains/concepts in Marloth, particularly in the [Dark forest](../Features/Dark%20forest%20181a3aae0f4b4056b6c28bb49e27978e.md) setting.

# Solution

> “Everyone [in a business team] needs to be an advocate for *something*.” - Phil Cowan

I have a two step solution for each nebulous domain:

1. Create a profession that specializes in that domain
2. Create one or more characters with that profession, and who are heavily identified *by* that profession, potentially definitively (The so-and-so).

# Ordinary extraordinary professions

Here I can borrow from my designs from my Marloth Game [](https://www.notion.so/b074627c12c5431c84522e9f7ec4bdab?pvs=21).

My goal with the various Marloth games I have created the past decade have been to take ordinary Victorian professions and fantasize them similar to the way D&D and related media gradually established thematic lore and mechanics for various medieval professions (cleric, bard, ranger).
