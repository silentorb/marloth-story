# Community

The essence of Folklore is derived from [[c80ee480543c42eda65e330b6d1c6d9b]].

Folklore is a way of establishing and preserving the culture of a community.

It sets guidelines and gives examples that are intended to encourage people to live wisely and responsibly.

# People

Folklore is about people, and human nature.

Folklore features mechanics that reflect wicked living and mechanics that reflect righteous living.
