# Big Trouble in Little China

One of my favorite movies.  It is a relentless stream of consciousness that seamlessly flows all over the place from one scene to the next.

> 💡 I listed this as an inspiration for [Peace in the eye of the storm](./4e21b1585595437d834a897e603fb87a.md), but this is only a partial inspiration.  I’m linking it because even when terrible and dark things are happening, it still manages to stay light and fun.
