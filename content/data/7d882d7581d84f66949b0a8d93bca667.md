# James submitted to Tralvorkemen

I’ve been throwing around the idea of having James being a subcontractor for [The Agency](../Features/The%20Agency%2044db7ec455e14b10a9bc1da088f20f66.md), with [Edward Tralvorkemen](../Data/Characters/Edward%20Tralvorkemen%2027b58e628ba2802cac4fdedacf73cf7d.md) being his actual employer.

I like the idea of this being an implicit background thread, without Tralvorkemen ever explicitly mentioned.

For most of the story, the audience can settle into a sense that James directly works for the Agency, with occasional hints briefly disrupting that sense.

[The Bear](../Data/Characters/The%20Bear%2095e68e70d28b409281fa2b931537bb79.md) is aware of the business relationship and occasionally refers to James’ employer.

[The Bear](../Data/Characters/The%20Bear%2095e68e70d28b409281fa2b931537bb79.md) has a healthy fear and deference to [Edward Tralvorkemen](../Data/Characters/Edward%20Tralvorkemen%2027b58e628ba2802cac4fdedacf73cf7d.md).

# Stewardship

For the most part, I want James to be a good example of submitting to Tralvorkemen. ([Good stewardship](../Features/Good%20stewardship%2023758e628ba28056a250f0c320c9c247.md))
