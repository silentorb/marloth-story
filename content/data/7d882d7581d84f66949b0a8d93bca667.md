# James submitted to Tralvorkemen

I’ve been throwing around the idea of having James being a subcontractor for [[44db7ec455e14b10a9bc1da088f20f66]], with [[27b58e628ba2802cac4fdedacf73cf7d]] being his actual employer.

I like the idea of this being an implicit background thread, without Tralvorkemen ever explicitly mentioned.

For most of the story, the audience can settle into a sense that James directly works for the Agency, with occasional hints briefly disrupting that sense.

[[95e68e70d28b409281fa2b931537bb79]] is aware of the business relationship and occasionally refers to James’ employer.

[[95e68e70d28b409281fa2b931537bb79]] has a healthy fear and deference to [[27b58e628ba2802cac4fdedacf73cf7d]].
