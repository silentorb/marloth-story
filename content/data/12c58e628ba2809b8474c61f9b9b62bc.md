# Magic in the background

Magic has become one of the primary themes of TWOLD, and it is starting to upstage other important themes.

It is fine for magic to be a prominent topic, but I want to move it to the background.

Similar to money, the world can be running around chasing it madly, but I want the main characters in the foreground to be concerned about other things.
