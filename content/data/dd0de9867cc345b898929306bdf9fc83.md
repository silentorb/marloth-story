Features are intended to be prevalent, defining, high level patterns.

While Features have a priority, I'm increasingly moving low priority features to [[528384943746443a9c89699b57e3bbec]].

Features should be general and independent of particular Marloth elements.

I am frequently associating features with [[2eea538996934ce8abafc27132e576c1]], so part of the motivation for these boundaries is having a tight feature list with minimal noise to connect to inspirations.
