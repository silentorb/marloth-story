# Samantha

# A helpful friend

I haven’t put a lot of thought into this character so far, even though she will be a perfect sounding board and companion for Adelle.

Which is okay, because that’s kind of how the dolls played out in the first book.  They were originally one-off characters.  But they just kept finding their way into more and more scenes until *I* found they had become major characters.

Sometimes the best things aren’t the divas that require inordinate pampering before ROI, its the things that keep giving even after you’ve forgotten about them.
