# The Raven

Aside for a few occasional angles, I haven’t been that interested in bringing the Raven back for the second book, but part of me wants to bring him back just to say that one line,

> “What I am about to tell you is astounding.  What I won’t tell you is even more astounding.”

I think part of the problem is I kind of feel like I tapped out the Raven.  If I brought him back it would be to do the same things and deliver the same lines as before.
