# The Necromancers

# Overview

A society of magic users who raise zombies.

# Motivation

The main initial impetus for this group is to give magic a bad name.  The Necromancers are an easy target to point out how bad magic is, and are focused on by the [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXY8X]] .

For the first few parts of TWOLD, this group is the primary antagonist of [[01KWN86X6MFZQAJ1V36T9592G6]].
