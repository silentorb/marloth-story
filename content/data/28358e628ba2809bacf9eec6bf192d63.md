# Contrived survival horror

Contrived survival horror is a sub-genre of [Survival horror](../Survival%20horror%20dc101b6438cf43a8b5f1e17212b8c950.md).

Contrived survival horror contains all of the requirements of [Survival horror](../Survival%20horror%20dc101b6438cf43a8b5f1e17212b8c950.md) , plus the following additions:

1. Contrived circumstances
2. Immediate dangers
3. Puzzles / problems that reward critical thinking

‣ is more of a marathon, while contrived survival horror is more of a sprint.

> 💡 ‣ is easier to mix in with other storylines and can be in the background.
> A contrived survival horror scenario generally needs to be the focal point of the story, or at least until the particular scenario is concluded.
