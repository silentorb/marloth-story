Cases where characters place other characters in malicious, contrived situations.

Contrived survival horror is a sub-genre of [[dc101b6438cf43a8b5f1e17212b8c950]].

Contrived survival horror contains all of the requirements of [[dc101b6438cf43a8b5f1e17212b8c950]] , plus the following additions:

1. Contrived circumstances
2. Immediate dangers
3. Puzzles / problems that reward critical thinking

[[28358e628ba280859907dafca337f880]] is more of a marathon, while contrived survival horror is more of a sprint.

> 💡 [[28358e628ba280859907dafca337f880]] is easier to mix with other storylines and can be in the background.
>
> A contrived survival horror scenario generally needs to be the focal point of the story, or at least until the particular scenario is concluded.

# Requirements

The situations:

1. Are dangerous.
2. Are some form of test, often testing both aptitude and moral decisions.
3. Usually involve some degree of reduced freedom such as imprisonment.
4. Are mind-bending.
5. Express a wicked sense of humor and irony.
6. Drive the subjects toward wickedness.
