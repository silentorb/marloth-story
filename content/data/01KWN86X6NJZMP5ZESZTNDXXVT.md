# Magic lights job

Mass producing magical devices that emit light.

The motivation for this product is the many dangers which are [[01KWN86X6MFZQAJ1V36T9592GH]].

# Simple job

I think it would be good to have the lights be a relatively simple job compared to the other projects.

This is a nod to D&D and other RPGs, where “Light” is a beginner spell.

# First to market

[[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXY36]] can present the project as a rush job where they are trying to get to market first.

# Rush job

Being first-to-market is overrated.

Slow-and-steady wins the race.

I like the idea of having James given an unrealistic deadline, and being forced to ship prototypes.

The prototypes sell well, but the [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXXVR]].

# Competition

The [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXXVQ]] later releases a stable alternative light product that ends up making a better profit.
