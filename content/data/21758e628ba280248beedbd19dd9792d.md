# Motivation and goals

This will be the main home for [[14f6a0f77a694f4cb18881bb58846c94]] and [[2ff1980fd10b42a7bc0f65b61a2ab3e8]] in TWOLD.

Why do I want them to have a home?

The primary motivations/goals for the forest home are:

1. [Wholesome marriage is beautiful](./21758e628ba280248beedbd19dd9792d.md) 
2. [Cozy horror](./21758e628ba280248beedbd19dd9792d.md) 

# Wholesome marriage is beautiful

Having a home is wholesome.  ([[729f8c8cb3774419a3611b8961a5da02]])

I want them to have adventures, but to do so in a mature and responsible manner, with a home as their physical anchor point.

While the couple will occasionally have guests, the home is primarily about the couple.  It should be designed to focus on and enhance the storytelling of their relationship.

# Cozy horror

The forest home will be James’ and Adelle’s primary sanctuary in TWOLD, and the primary focal point for [[e5cc80dc61ed4c629951cdf472b20b7a]].

That means there will need to be many dangers lurking just beyond their walls.
