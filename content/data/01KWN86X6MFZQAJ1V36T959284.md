# No desperate trying to stop a big bad from appearing

There should never be a scenario where there is a powerful malicious force that is unstoppable, and the only protection is to ensure the world never has to face that force.

The world doesn’t work like that.  Power doesn’t work like that.  It’s a misleading scenario.

> 💡 There can still be plenty of ongoing defense, protecting physical borders from invaders and protecting supernatural borders from otherworldly invaders.
