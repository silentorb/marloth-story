# No super characters

The idea has become increasingly popular that humans can gain intrinsic powers far beyond their peers.

That idea is humanistic and naïve.

At times I have wanted to counter it, but increasingly I have found that the best solution is to ignore it.  Show people a world where super powers are not an issue.  Clear the air and focus on other issues.
