# Hospitality

This is closely related to [[e5cc80dc61ed4c629951cdf472b20b7a]], but emphasizes the loving aspect of it.

The point of this feature is for characters to lovingly provide food and shelter to those in need.

The service can still be commercial, but it should always be a generous commercial provision.
