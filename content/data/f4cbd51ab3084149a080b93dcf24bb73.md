# James and Miles

Just as Edward Tralvorkemen and Jakob Damond waged a battle-of-the-wits war behind the scenes of the first book, [James](./14f6a0f77a694f4cb18881bb58846c94.md) and [Miles](./55be668bc999441da2a96ebd11a05626.md) wage a similar war behind the scenes in TWOLD.

# Save the world

At the root of their contention is Miles’ belief that he can save fix the world.  [Save the world](./f60f7905c81b43cb94e8c0b36feba57a.md).

Miles is striving to save the world, and James knows it is beyond his control.

# Contrast

Even though Miles is wrong, his stance can appear more sympathetic to some, and cast doubt on James’ stance.  James looks passive and apathetic compared to Miles’ “do whatever it takes” attitude.

> “You’re not going to break the rules?  You clearly don’t care enough.”

> “We don’t stand a chance unless we harness the darkness.”
