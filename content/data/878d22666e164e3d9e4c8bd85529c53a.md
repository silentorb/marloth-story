Commotion that is happening outside the immediate scene location.

## Motivation

Ambient action is a way of resolving the tension between action and storytelling.

# Neverwinter Nights

Many video games employ this technique, but the definitive one that most inspired this concept is [Neverwinter Nights](./b1de5c0c474b4a9f96d7b652dcd30ece.md).
