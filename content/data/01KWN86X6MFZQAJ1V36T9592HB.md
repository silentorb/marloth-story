# Overview

Sanctuaries provide the cozy in [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXY8M]].

Sanctuaries are safe and secure.

They invoke warmth—warm lighting, warm food, and warm drinks.

And windows to see the harsh conditions outside and emphasize the contrast.

# Contrast

The antithesis of sanctuaries is the [[01KWN86X6MFZQAJ1V36T9592HA]].

# Rules

## Friends

In most cases, whenever a major character is in a sanctuary, he or she should be in the company of friends.

## Food

At least some of the sanctuary scenes need [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXY1Y]].

## Industry

Sanctuaries should not simply be places of leisure.

They should involve rest but should also involve industry.

Not only do sanctuaries involve people preparing food and maintaining the sanctuary, there should be some degree of the people being served who are also working.

I may be mixing too much of my Millennial culture into this, but I still like the notion of people working at a desk while drinking a fancy beverage.

## Discussion

A significant portion of the discussion within sanctuaries should be about the dangerous world outside, both general discussion and planning next steps and future excursions.
