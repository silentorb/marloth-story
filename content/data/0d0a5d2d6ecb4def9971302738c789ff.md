# Contrasting stable and unstable characters

Stability is valuable, but it requires commitment, diligence, and contentment.

Some characters avoid stability because they do not want to pay the price.

However, all characters need what stability provides.

Show stable characters helping out unstable characters via [Hospitality](Hospitality%20cad51fd00b284dc094de3a19c1aed59c.md).
