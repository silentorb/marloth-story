---
title: The Maker and the makers
---
There are two sets of creators in the world: God and man.

God does not need man, yet God created man to in turn create.

Man's creations are lesser than God's creations, but man's creations still have some value.

# Harmony

There is a mysterious synergy between God's creation and man's creation.

The two forms of creation seem to compliment each other.

# Imbalance

Humans seem to fall into two ditches with this area.

One one end, is when humans rely too much on artificial methods and synthesis.

The end result becomes distorted and can contain hidden harm.

On the other end, people can idolize nature and treat it as something that shouldn't be touched.

They see harmony as leaving things alone, not actually *complimenting* each other.
