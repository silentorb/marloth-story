# James and Adelle are banished from the Asylum

# Less is more

## Problem

I’ve been wanting to have this grand adventure section where James and Adelle navigate through the [The City of Orphans](./79bb690186294ddfbd417bfcc108b5ac.md), but I’ve been wrestling for a long time with how to pull that off.

All of the ideas I’ve had for this have felt a little hollow.

The main problems are:

1. Lack of clear [Ideological contests](./3095477179ab4fd1a6977f151ff2aef8.md).
2. Lack of clear survival mechanics (how do they survive when others don’t?  What is the differentiator?)

## Solution

Leverage [Background protagonists](./35c58e628ba2807ea6a2d08791deb813.md) and [No explanation](./1ea58e628ba2800fb2d6d9e69acf85e2.md).

Don’t depict James and Adelle in the city at all.

Simply narrate them being kicked out of the Asylum and don’t even show what is on the other side of the doors.

> 💡 This also preserves the sense of mystery in [Questioning what is actually outside the Asylum](./24858e628ba280fd9cc8cb7f9fd46725.md).
