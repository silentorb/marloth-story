# Band of heroes vs. married couple

# Married Alice in Wonderland

## Background

For parts of the next two books I have wanted to have James and Adelle as a solo couple.

Instead of Alice wandering around Wonderland, have a married couple wandering around Wonderland.

## Thought experiment

What if I made that the bulk of the story?

I’m not seriously considering that possibility, but I want to explore it a little to better understand the boundaries around this problem domain.

## Elaborate network of relationships

My main issue with a solo couple is so much of the drama I am envisioning involves an elaborate network of relationships.

I want both James and Adelle to have a variety of relationships with a variety of characters.
