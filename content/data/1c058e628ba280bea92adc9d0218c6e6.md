# Dream system

The Dream system:

1. Is one of the [[1c058e628ba280b0b2e6c395dd9daddb]].
2. Is emotional, poetic, whimsical, and metaphorical.
3. Revolves around an innumerable number of interrelated dimensions .
4. Is synonymous with story book magic.
5. Has much overlap with the [[1c058e628ba280b9a9f0e23344e9ddc7]].

> 💡 Most characters are unaware of the Dream system.

# Madness

The Dream system is closely associated with madness and mind-screw storylines.

The Dream system is inherently disorienting, though there is “method to the madness”.

# Travelers

Travelers are people who are partially aware of multiple dimensions at once.

## Travelers and madness

Being torn between worlds, travelers are often effectively insane.

However, mature travelers can stabilize and exert limited control over the traveling.
