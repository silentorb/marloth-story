# Asylum job

I’m considering having James’ first depicted job be in the Asylum, but I’m not sure how to pull that off yet.

# Joseph

James has been in the Asylum before, and it might be cool if he could somehow leverage his past experience in dealing with other patients or some other aspect of the Asylum.

Originally when I first had this idea it was also in the context of a sort of “Joseph in prison”, where leaders would be impressed by his abilities and show him favor.

> 💡 These are difficult to realize ideas, but part of their appeal is they are so unconventional and unexpected.

# Resolution

This is a nice idea and I may revisit it, but whichever direction I take the Asylum, it probably won’t be this one.  The Asylum is increasingly becoming a visit and not a place I plan on lingering too long in this time around.
