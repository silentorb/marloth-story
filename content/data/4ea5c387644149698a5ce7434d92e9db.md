# Where does Fugue fit in?

Fugue was the #1 breakout character of the first book, but I’m not sure how he fits into the second story, and I don’t want to force him into it.

It doesn’t help that I don’t feel like he should ever work for the Bear.  They can’t interact on some level, but the Bear seems like a character Fugue wouldn’t put up with.

- If they do interact, I almost see them having a more antagonistic relationship.

> 💡 Resolution: I think I’ve found a way to introduce Fugue into the storyline and help out James while still maintaining animosity toward the bear.
