# Woke feminism

Woke feminism is the extreme feminism has taken.

Early feminism mostly sought equal rights, a complicated and mixed bag, but still simpler more benign than where it would lead.

But increasingly feminism became about making women more masculine.

Between feminism and other complimentary movements, women were gradually masculated and men were gradually emasculated, reaching parity in full woke.

Instead of championing the distinct qualities of womanhood, the woke solution to equality is to claim that women have no distinct qualities and women are basically men.

Ironically, woke feminism is anti-femininity.  It is only pro-female as long as females rejects femininity.
