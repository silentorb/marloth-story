# Millamer seeing Nivana at “work”

# Inspiration

Many years ago there was an article in World Magazine exploring the problems of sex workers.

The article contained an account of an exotic dancer who was performing when a male friend of hers walked into the establishment and their eyes met.  Both parties were embarrassed.

Since reading that article I have wanted to somehow incorporate that scene into one of my stories.
