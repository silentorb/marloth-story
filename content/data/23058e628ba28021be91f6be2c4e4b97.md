# The Gardener

The initial motivation for this character is to provide a profession ([Articulate domains by professions](../../Articles/Articulate%20domains%20by%20professions%2022f58e628ba280f3a94bf9a0d0460137.md)) to better articulate the vegetation aspect of [Dark forest](../../Features/Dark%20forest%20181a3aae0f4b4056b6c28bb49e27978e.md).

Partially derived from previous design work in the [Marloth Game](https://www.notion.so/Marloth-Game-57e5fdbb987949c0b2b69741a6a4bc08?pvs=21) character class, [Gardener](https://www.notion.so/Gardener-d5a8fbfde90b44db90185cf0bca13691?pvs=21).
