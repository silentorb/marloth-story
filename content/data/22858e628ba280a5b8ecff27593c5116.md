# James and Adelle behind-the-scenes discussions

# Overview

One feature I want to leverage with TWOLD is to have scenes where James and Adelle relate and discuss recent events in each other’s life.

Such scenes kill multiple birds with one stone.  They perform the following functions:

## Depict James and Adelle relating to each other.

In particular, depict James and Adelle relating to each other about day-to-day *life*.

## Depict James and Adelle being *open* with each other.

Too many romance stories revolve around secrets between the couple and people not being honest with each other.

## Depict James and Adelle confiding with and encouraging each other.

This implements [Suffering softened by marriage](Suffering%20softened%20by%20marriage%2040648921533242b3b7695a3f120931ee.md).

## Provide a behind-the-scenes perspective

This allows me to leave out details during events then provide James’ and Adelle’s perspective later.

> 💡 Helps counter the problem described in [Sense and Sensibility - The importance of visual suffering](../Articles/Suffering%20and%20Trials/Visual%20suffering/Sense%20and%20Sensibility%20-%20The%20importance%20of%20visual%20s%2022858e628ba2804fbda3e9cc4dbacf47.md).

> 💡 Doing this also helps reduce [Repeated recounting](Repeated%20recounting%2022858e628ba28066a2d2d4183a892eb5.md).
> With that said:
> 1. This recounting will still involve some information duplication
> 2. I want to push the boundaries of how much information I can duplicate since I really like recounting. ([Repeated recounting](Repeated%20recounting%2022858e628ba28066a2d2d4183a892eb5.md).)
