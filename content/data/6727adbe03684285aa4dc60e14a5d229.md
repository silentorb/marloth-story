# Guest consultant

Relating to [Sojourner](./1e2cf9c7997f48f7bf052c4ee98aec4b.md), James should regularly step into other organizations and settings as a helper and guest.

In such cases, the natives have local knowledge James lacks, and James has global knowledge the natives lack.

> 💡 This idea is primarily inspired by my own work experiences.  I have rarely seen it in media.  ‣

> 💡 The inspirations I have linked to this feature only faintly reflect the feature.  If it wasn’t for my personal experiences, I would have never identified that such a pattern exists or that those inspirations contain any of that pattern.
