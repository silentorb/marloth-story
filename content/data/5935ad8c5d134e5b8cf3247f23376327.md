# Limited spiritual resources

What if there was a limited resource that people needed to consume like food, and without it they would lose their ability to do good?

What would that resource be?

How would it be acquired?

What would happen without it?

Off the top of my head, I see two potential consequences:

1. Turn into a monster
2. Go insane

These options aren’t mutually exclusive.  The setting can support both inflictions.

# Nature of the resource

The resource should be from beyond the immediate world.
