# Parable character

A character who is used as an object lesson involving moral decisions.

Parable characters are usually tragic and demonstrate what a person *shouldn’t* do.

> 💡 Parable characters are simpler than [[1a758e628ba28093bb2ce20c597c8638]]s, while their souls and motivations are explored more than [[1a758e628ba2806bba7ec053001f2dd0]]s.
