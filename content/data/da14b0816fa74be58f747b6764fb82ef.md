# James and Adelle awake in the Asylum

Adelle carefully studied her surroundings.

“It’s rather dreary,” said Adelle.

“Yes, it’s the Asylum.”

“I know, but you could have given the room a *little* attention.”

“I can’t imagine what could be done to it.”

“No, I suppose not.”

Adelle continued looking around.  “At least there’s no posters of girls,” she said.

“Excuse me?”
