# Senses - Survival horror mechanics

# Overview

Out of the five senses, creatures rarely use touch and taste to detect other creatures.

That leaves the following senses:

[[01KWN86X6MFZQAJ1V36T9592HE]]

1. Sight
2. Smell
3. Sound
4. Sixth sense (supernatural)
5. Devices (both scientific and magical)
