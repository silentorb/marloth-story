# Overview

At any given point in time, a Marloth narrative is in one of three modes:

1. [[01KWN86X6MFZQAJ1V36T9592GQ]]
2. Tense
3. [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXY2P]]

In general, each scene should fall under one of those three categories.

# Composition

Cozy should rarely transition straight to or from cozy.

Tense is the transition between cozy and action.

Action should be less common than cozy and tense.

Similar to music rhythm, the flow should be something like:

> cozy -> tense -> cozy -> Tense -> Action! -> Tense -> cozy -> tense -> etc.
