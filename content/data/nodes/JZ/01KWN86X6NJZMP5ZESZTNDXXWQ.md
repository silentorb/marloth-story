# Nivana and the innkeeper

# Matchmaking rematch

In moving away from Miles and Nivana getting married ([Speak now, or forever hold your peace](./01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXXWT.md)), I’m now leaning toward a different marital arrangement.

Instead of Millamer, I think Nivana would benefit from a gentler, humbler man.


I’d be a little more hesitant to create a character just to provide a companion for Nivana, except he would also help round out a lot of what I’m trying to do with the story in general.

# Drenodir

I keep trying to shoehorn [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXY32]] into the role of an innkeeper, and he just doesn’t fit.  Drenodir has a restless, warrior side.  I need a character with none of that.

Drenodir does still fit the setting, and I could see him and his wife possibly working *for* the innkeeper.  The innkeeper would be the anchoring force of stability, allowing the Drenodirs some flexibility to roam around the story a bit.
