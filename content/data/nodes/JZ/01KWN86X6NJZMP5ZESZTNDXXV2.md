# Folklore, religion, and the supernatural

I’m realizing that what I have been searching for in folklore is intrinsically connected to religion and supernatural phenomena.

There are two main steps which connect these three ideas and form this recipe:

1. Folklore → Religion: Folklore isn’t simply quaint notions, its tied to core belief systems and deities
2. Religion → Supernatural phenomena: An essential part of the appeal—especially in a fantasy story—is how the religious features affect every day life, via supernatural occurrences.
