# Travelers

Lucid dreamers.

People who are partially aware of the multiple overlapping dreams and have a challenging time distinguishing between them.

Travelers have a better memory of past dreams and a worse memory of current dreams.

Inexperienced travelers often appear insane (and in one sense are insane.)

Expert travelers are better at distinguishing and remembering dreams.
