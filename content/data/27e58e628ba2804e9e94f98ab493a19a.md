# Mr. Mosspuddle printing copies of Marloth

For a long time considered the idea of having [Mr. Mosspuddle](../Data/Characters/Mr%20Mosspuddle%2027e58e628ba28079b3dbea177182d1f9.md) going Gutenberg on Marloth and mass distributing it.

I don’t have any ideas with how to run with it and haven’t thought about it in a while, but it’s an easy idea to slip into the narrative and maybe more extensions to it will hit me once I start implementing it.
