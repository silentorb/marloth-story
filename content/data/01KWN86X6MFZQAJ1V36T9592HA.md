# Ingredients

The ingredients of a cozy horror wilderness setting are:

1. Travel
2. Industry
3. Dangers
4. Isolation

# Industry

Sometimes characters must perform activities within the wilderness.

In those cases, the character isn’t so much traveling between sanctuaries as traveling to a site, working there, then returning to a sanctuary.

> 💡 The longer the duration of the work activity, the greater the danger.

# Dangers

The cozy horror wilderness dangers are a subset of [[01KWN86X6MFZQAJ1V36T9592H7]].

That is because most cozy horror dangers are [[01KWN86X6NJZMP5ZESZTNDXY1N]] dangers.

> 💡For example, hunger is *not* a major concern when traveling in the wilderness.
>
> When spending several days at a time in the wilderness, hunger can be a greater concern, but so are all of the other dangers.
