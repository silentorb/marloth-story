# No primary villain

> 💡 Related to [[768270621ce349f0b328cedfdd4e8474]].

Marloth books should not have a single villain that the majority of villains report to.

The sense of villainy and malice should be blurred.  The audience should feel threatened, but not by any single entity.

They can have primary masterminds that are indirectly manipulating the other villains.

> 💡 This relates to [Impersonal organizations](./2aa58e628ba280a19372f46ef56816ca.md).
