# Priest

A religious representative that is initially [Nivana](Nivana%20a2ee9cd0a28b4945af74fd3022fd97af.md)’s friend and assists her with the [Campaign against magic](../../TWOLD%20Plot/Arcs/Campaign%20against%20magic%20e7a911ec8497468eb29b6238f24b06a4.md).

He rejects Nivana once she is accused of practicing magic.
