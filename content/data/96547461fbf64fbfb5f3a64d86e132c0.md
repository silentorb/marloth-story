# Unforgiving - A Northern Hymn

> 💡 *Unforgiving* contains some [[ce5b83035c894e0da2137f0584297c4d]] scenes and elements, but most of those feel tacked on and incongruent to the rest of the narrative.
> Part of the problem is the game lacks introspection.  It has some elements that suggest the designers were intending to have some introspection but didn’t know how to integrate it.
