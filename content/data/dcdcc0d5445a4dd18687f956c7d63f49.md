# Super-human

> 💡 I don’t want this to be a foil anymore.  I don’t want this in the story.  Sometimes the best counter is omission.

Other than Tralvorkemen, I don’t want TWOLD to have any super-humans.

What I am interested in are characters who desire to be super-human, and maybe cases where characters briefly appear to be super-human, only to have tragic falls.
