# Nivana in the Mediev garden

Introduce the Gardener.

## Brainstorming idea

I would like the Gardener doing something both meaningful and also something I can relate to with my limited horticulture.

In my own life, I've been regularly bemoaning how the trees in our neighborhood are being cut down and rarely replaced with new saplings.

Once fertile streets are turning into a desert.

Maybe the Gardener could be planting a new tree where a previous tree died.
