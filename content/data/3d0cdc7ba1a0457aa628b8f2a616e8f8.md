Blending natural countryside with civilized constructions.

# Natural

On the natural side, this includes:

* Vegetation (trees, bushes, grass, vines)

* Water (stream, ponds, waterfalls)

* Earth (rocks, dirt, sand)

# Constructions

* Buildings

* Roads

* Walls

* Lamps

* Signs

* Utilities (wells, windmills, fountains)

# Personal preference

Pure nature is beautiful, but for me there is something missing from it.

Pure man-made settings feel sterile and lack a certain vibrance only nature provides.
