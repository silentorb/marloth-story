# Magic lights job

Mass producing magical devices that emit light.

The motivation for this product is the many dangers which are [Vulnerable to light](../Features/Vulnerable%20to%20light%2027c58e628ba280f3aa0af9365c7362c5.md).

# Simple job

I think it would be good to have the lights be a relatively simple job compared to the other projects.

This is a nod to D&D and other RPGs, where “Light” is a beginner spell.

# First to market

[The Bear](../Data/Characters/The%20Bear%2095e68e70d28b409281fa2b931537bb79.md) can present the project as a rush job where they are trying to get to market first.

# Rush job

Being first-to-market is overrated.

Slow-and-steady wins the race.

I like the idea of having James given an unrealistic deadline, and being forced to ship prototypes.

The prototypes sell well, but the [Lights catch on fire](Lights%20catch%20on%20fire%202e058e628ba280adab44f7732cc87820.md).

# Competition

The [Quality Tech company](../Data/Groups/Quality%20Tech%20company%202e058e628ba28072b6cbc427d55c509c.md) later releases a stable alternative light product that ends up making a better profit.

“They used our lights as prototypes!”

“They did, and you did as well.  You sold prototypes and made a profit from it.”
