# Hell is about to break loose in the Asylum

“Where is my supply case?”

“Supply case?  Do you mean that box behind the bed?”

James looked behind the bed.

“Yes, that’s the one.  Thank you!”

James opened the case, took out two pistols, and began to load them.

Adelle was shocked.  “James! What on earth are those for?”

James looked up from the pistols and said, “Risk Mitigation.”
