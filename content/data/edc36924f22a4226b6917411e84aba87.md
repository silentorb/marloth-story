# Reading Marloth changes people

It doesn’t happen to everyone, but characters who read the narrative representation of Marloth tend to be convicted, and have their eyes opened to a larger, scarier, and more wonderful world.

In such cases, reading Marloth is like opening Pandora’s box—the character’s life and perception of life is upended and shaken.

Things start to get real crazy.
