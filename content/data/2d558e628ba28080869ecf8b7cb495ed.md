# Heart

I want Marloth stories to have a lot of drama, but not any kind of drama.

I’m not interested in neurotic drama (though I can’t help having a little of it.)

I’m not interested in melodrama (though I can’t help having a little of it.)

The closest word I’ve found to describe the kind of drama I want is drama *with heart*.

Drama that is poignant and beautiful.

Drama that exhibits fruit of the Spirit.
