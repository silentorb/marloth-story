# Commuting to the Asylum

A taxi arrives outside.

The taxi driver is shooting zombie-like creatures while the passengers are boarding.

> 💡 There are one or two other contract engineers driving in the back of the car as well.

[One of the engineers] said “Hey, it’s [Such and such’s]!  That place has the best [Some kind of] food in the City!  Have any of you been there?”

“I haven’t,” said Grihulone, but maybe we can go there for lunch today.”

Occasionally they would pass similar taxis driving about the city.

At one point they came upon a small bus.  It was parked by a corner as passengers exited and entered.

The cab pulled up beside the bus and the two drivers nodded to each other.

“Hamish.”

“Egger.”

“Beware the East Quarter,” said the bus driver, “‘Tis overrun since midnight.”

“The usual lot?”

“Aye.”

The drivers each nodded again, and the cab resumed its journey.
