# Scientists

# Overview

Scientists are a major faction in Marloth.  Scientists are mostly comprised of doctors, but the composition may eventually diversify into other professions.

# Cosmic horror

Scientists are the primary explorers of [[7015e32dadb042b995663e84d5b48388]].  This generally involves opening Pandora’s box and introducing all sorts of misfortune upon the world.

# The Asylum

[[84234f68b64e4530836706622d4bde1b]] is one of the primary locations for doctors.
