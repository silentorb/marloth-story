# The Marloth technical system

# Subsystems

The Marloth technical system is divided into three subsystems:

[Science system](The%20Marloth%20technical%20system/Science%20system%201c058e628ba28031b77dc6265b5aeeea.md)

[Wizardry system](The%20Marloth%20technical%20system/Wizardry%20system%201c058e628ba280b9a9f0e23344e9ddc7.md)

[Dream system](The%20Marloth%20technical%20system/Dream%20system%201c058e628ba280bea92adc9d0218c6e6.md)

Science and Wizardry are the two prominent systems.

Dream is equally important but more behind-the-scenes.

> 💡 The denizens of Marloth are less aware of the dream system.
