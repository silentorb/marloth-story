# Extended, fresh continuity

The plot of the narrative should be:

| Extended | The scope of a single book feels like multiple books or a season of a [TV series](../../Inspirations/Inspiration%20types/TV%20series%20c847c77114e94ca5ba74405c2a088c76.md) |
| --- | --- |
| Fresh | Continually mix things up and not rely heavily on repeated templates |
| Continuity | Seamlessly transition without feeling like the plot has stopped and started a whole new story |
