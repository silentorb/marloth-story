# Labyrinth

> 💡 Technically this has a [[f2ec9bb510dc440b96920e4651a259be]], but the city in Labyrinth doesn’t actually feel *lived in*, and it only feels like *posturing* danger.

# Wonderland

Labyrinth is a mixed bag that exhibits the [[3cbc40d2ba2a4c76b4b9dc370452fcfe]] pattern but I wouldn’t consider it a great example of Wonderland.

I’m still noting *Labyrinth* here because there are other aspects of the movie which have been useful influences on Marloth.

# Mimicry

One of my main complaints with Labyrinth is I feel like it is parroting Alice in Wonderland without the same understanding and brilliance.
