# Members of an unpopular affiliation

I like the idea of having James and Adelle (particularly James) being a part of some group of people that many characters look down on.

The requirements for this feature are:

1. It should be vague.
2. A character should never say they are a part of the group.
3. Characters should be able to discern that a person is a part of the group simply by looking at them.
    1. There should never be any hint as to how people can discern that.
4. The group should involve religious beliefs.
5.
