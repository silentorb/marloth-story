# Adelle as a Seamstress

For a long time, I’ve envisioned adult Adelle as a part-time seamstress.

# Motivation

The main reasons for Adelle being a seamstress are:

1. It is a flexible job that can be done from home and doesn’t conflict with housework.
2. It ties back to her being taught how to sew by Miss Bethany as a child.
3. My wife likes to sew and has a sewing/craft room.
