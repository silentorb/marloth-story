# James and Adelle - unlocked door

Adelle looked at the door.

“Maybe it’s unlocked.”

“It can’t be unlocked.  This is a containment cell.  The point is to prevent us from leaving the room on our own.”

“This door has a handle on our side.  Isn’t that odd for a containment cell?  Did your door have a knob before?”

“I don’t remember.  Much of the time I was barely conscious.”

Adelle turned the knob and the door opened outward.  She peered through the doorway and then back to James.

“It’s not locked.”

“Yes, that’s why I was saying—wait, *what?*”

“The door is unlocked.”

James paused for a moment and then said, “I would have tried the door *eventually*.”

Adelle smiled and peered through the doorway again.  James moved closer and looked with her.  It was a dark hallway with many doors, just as he remembered it.  

“Let’s go exploring!” said Adelle.
