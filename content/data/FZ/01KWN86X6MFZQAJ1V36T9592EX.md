# Overview

One feature I want to leverage with TWOLD is to have scenes where James and Adelle relate and discuss recent events in each other’s life.

Such scenes kill multiple birds with one stone.  They perform the following functions:

In particular, depict James and Adelle relating to each other about day-to-day *life*.

## Depict James and Adelle being *open* with each other.

Too many romance stories revolve around secrets between the couple and people not being honest with each other.
