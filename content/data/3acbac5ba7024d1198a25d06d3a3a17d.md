# George applies to the Wizards’ Council

# Design

This is one of those early ideas which has hung around for years, and is dear to my heart, but feels a little dated.

However, it does relate to many of the themes and later events of the story.  In a way, it is a good introduction to the existence of the organization of Wizardry.

And [George](../Data/Characters/George%20e22279389f1e493abd308e948d47a2e8.md)  has a real place in the story.  I’m still not where all I will use him, simply because the story has room for *many* George’s, and I don’t want to overuse him.
