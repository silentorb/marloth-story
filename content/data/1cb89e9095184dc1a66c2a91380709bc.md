# George and Desa arcs

There are some parallels between these two arcs:

- [George - Science to magic](../../TWOLD%20Plot/Arcs/George%20-%20Science%20to%20magic%203dad6c0d084840b9852f5255e1486f22.md) arc
- [Desa arc](../../TWOLD%20Plot/Arcs/Desa%20arc%20806f96cbb4794b38801823e008aee1b8.md)

Both involve outcasts from intellectual societies, one scientific and one magical.  Both characters become mavericks.

> 💡 There may be an opportunity for these two characters to cross paths, mutually sympathize, and collaborate.
