# Spectacles

Every action scene should include at least one (and usually just one) [Spectacles](./35c58e628ba280b191dffa33400b7c59.md).

Within the context of Marloth narratives, I'm considering whether action and spectacles are practically synonymous and it is potentially redundant to treat them as distinct.
