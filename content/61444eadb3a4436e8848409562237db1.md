# Uncertified magic experts

> 💡 Related to [Rag tag band of friends](../Features/Rag%20tag%20band%20of%20friends%209092ace4d11741659a7633ee2707f0a9.md) but this isn’t necessarily rag tag and doesn’t count toward fulfilling that requirement.

I like the idea of [James](../Data/Characters/James%2014f6a0f77a694f4cb18881bb58846c94.md) being a part of a middle class of contractors who specialize in magic, but lack credentials and respect.

This class is primarily contrasted with the two competing schools of tech, the Wizards and the Scientists.  Both schools look down on the lowly magic practitioners.

However, the lowly magic practitioners are more economical and can be afforded by parties that could never afford the accredited organizations.
