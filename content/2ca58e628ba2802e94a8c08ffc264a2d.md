# The Watching Door

A supernatural door with an eye on it.

A mysterious force with a mysterious agenda.

The only clear aspect of that agenda is flooding the world with horrors.

# Design origin

I originally created the Watching Door in my teens in a comic series I started that never went very far with.

The main character was a short, mustached, European detective that specialized in the occult.

I’m not sure what originally inspired the idea.  Later in life I discovered many similar characters and story structures but wasn’t aware of them in my teens.
