# Desperate industry

# Overview

The industrious job angle is coming together well, except with it I am getting away from the survival and [Desperation](../Marloth/Features/Desperation%203c8bb63bea1440cba5eb8b570cecec24.md).

Much of industry and innovation is a luxury.

How is impetus normally placed upon creativity in narrative?

# War time impetus

The main innovative impetus I can think of is people trying to make a new military technology during a war.

But that’s the kind of standard epic impetus I’m trying to avoid.

The main part of this job is it is supposed to be lowly, with a splash of romantic wonder, not “[Save the world](../Marloth/Archive/Foils/Save%20the%20world%20f60f7905c81b43cb94e8c0b36feba57a.md)”.

# Further thoughts

However, the previous section does provide an additional boundary that helps further define the issues here.

If one of the few narrative impetuses for innovation is war, it further emphasizes how much innovation is a luxory during [Desperation](../Marloth/Features/Desperation%203c8bb63bea1440cba5eb8b570cecec24.md).
