# One or two countrysides?

So I’m thinking of having the forest be a [Jane Austen countryside](jane-austen-countryside-13758e628ba280f28cd6d54f56e04bc4.md), and then I remember that the Mediev Manor in the first part was supposed to be in a similar setting.

The difference is I had never really fleshed out the surrounding area of the Mediev Manor, and always wanted it to be close to the Nameless City, possibly close enough for the City to be visible from the Manor.

Should I have two countrysides, or should I merge them into one?

Or maybe I can apply the [Tale of two cities](tale-of-two-cities-4ad86022c46e49e9843a4c05ab0d3bcd.md) idea to the countryside, and have two countrysides that are actually two sides of the same coin?
