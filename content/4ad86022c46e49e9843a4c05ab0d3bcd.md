# Tale of two cities

Through much of TWOLD’s design process, I’ve envisioned having two cities, that are actually two angles (and maybe different stages) of the same city.

The two cities are [The Nameless City](../Data/Locations/The%20Nameless%20City%202639e47762d1483c9c8619c59575b713.md) and [The City of Orphans](../Data/Locations/The%20City%20of%20Orphans%2079bb690186294ddfbd417bfcc108b5ac.md).

It’s generally been a non-essential idea, but it seems easy to fit into most of the other elements and is useful.

Biblically, it seems like a great way to subtly overlay some of the prophetic themes of Israel, where the Nameless City is 2 Samuel era, and the City of Orphans is Ezekiel era.
