# Fighting against the magic system

[Products: TWOLD](twold-e028aa0786f5449984a4f497c1d746fa.md)

# Overview

I’ve unwittingly created many storylines involving characters fighting against the status quo of magic use.

Each storylines seems to be unique and have fresh value independent of the others, so I don’t see any danger of redundancy.

If anything, I think it maybe good to unify these related storylines and potentially have them culminate toward a single point.

# Scope

The scope of this topic is people feeling oppressed by the magical society they are living in, and trying to change things.

They generally feel oppressed because they do not wield enough magical power.  The magical power they lack can take both the form of intrinsic magical ability, and extrinsic political power with magical hierarchies.

<aside>
💡 I would categorize [Nivana champions anti-magic](nivana-champions-anti-magic-0a430a0a2ad84e54a4c65463c2e8d4cc.md) outside of this subject.  That arc is about someone trying to remove magic from a largely non-magical society.

However, it may still be worth tying the two arcs together in places because there are some corollaries.

</aside>
