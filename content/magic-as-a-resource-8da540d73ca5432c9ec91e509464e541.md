# Magic as a resource

One of the main fantasy mechanics to come from my earlier Nivana plot lines is the idea of Magic energy being a highly prized resource that certain individuals will go to extreme lengths to acquire.

Magic from humans

Some humans are natural reservoirs of magic.

Magical energy can only be drained from men by killing them.

Magical energy can be drained from women without killing them.
