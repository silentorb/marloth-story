# Glory

In TWOLD, the Nameless city is the bastion of human prosperity and progress.

It is also hypocritical—with a pompous outside and a deviant core.

# War

For the first few parts of TWOLD, this group is the primary antagonist of [The Necromancers](the-necromancers-5fe18fead3844cea92136ea65ede0940.md).

For later parts, there may be war between the Nameless City and [The City of Orphans](the-city-of-orphans-79bb690186294ddfbd417bfcc108b5ac.md).
