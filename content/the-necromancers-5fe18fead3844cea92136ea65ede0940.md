# The Necromancers

[Parents: Magicians](magicians-993b14e4e8e54ed8b690f56cea8429a2.md)

# Overview

A society of magic users who raise zombies.

# Motivation

The main initial impetus for this group is to give magic a bad name.  The Necromancers are an easy target to point out how bad magic is, and are focused on by the [Campaign against magic](../../TWOLD%20Plot/Arcs/Campaign%20against%20magic%20e7a911ec8497468eb29b6238f24b06a4.md) .

For the first few parts of TWOLD, this group is the primary antagonist of [The Nameless City](the-nameless-city-2639e47762d1483c9c8619c59575b713.md).
