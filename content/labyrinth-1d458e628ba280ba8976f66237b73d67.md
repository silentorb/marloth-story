# Wonderland

Labyrinth is a mixed bag that exhibits the [Wonderland](wonderland-3cbc40d2ba2a4c76b4b9dc370452fcfe.md) pattern but I wouldn’t consider it a great example of Wonderland.

I’m still noting *Labyrinth* here because there are other aspects of the movie which have been useful influences on Marloth.

# Mimicry

One of my main complaints with Labyrinth is I feel like it is parroting Alice in Wonderland without the same understanding and brilliance.
