# The City vs. the Necromancers

The [The Nameless City](../../Data/Locations/The%20Nameless%20City%202639e47762d1483c9c8619c59575b713.md) and the [The Necromancers](../../Data/Groups/The%20Necromancers%205fe18fead3844cea92136ea65ede0940.md) are at war with each other.

> 💡 In some respects, this is a macro version of the Orphanage scenario in the first book, where leadership is fanatically striving to keep evil at bay, with some justification.
