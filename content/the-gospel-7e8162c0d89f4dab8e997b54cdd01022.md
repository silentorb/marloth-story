# The Gospel

One of the core features of the Marloth setting is the Gospel.  Where glimmers of light breaking into a small, darkened world.  Where everything that is good and right is beyond, and only through benevolent condescension can that good be introduced to the internal occupants.

One of the main applications of this feature is [Windows](windows-6b5b7ddc53744c5ab0699082e495ea88.md).
