# The Asylum spanning two cities

[Parents: Tale of two cities](tale-of-two-cities-4ad86022c46e49e9843a4c05ab0d3bcd.md)
[Products: TWOLD](twold-e028aa0786f5449984a4f497c1d746fa.md)

I’ve always wrestled with which city is the home of the Asylum.

Maybe it can be in both cities?

I could have it be in the City of Orphans initially, and then mention it being in the Nameless City during the final climax as a hint that the cities are actually the one and the same.

<aside>
💡

Like a lot of my surrealism, the trick would be doing it in such a way that readers didn’t think I just made a mistake.
I expect what I’m thinking of will make people go, “Wait, what?”  There’s a fine line between that working and not working, and I’m not entirely sure yet how I would make this work…

</aside>
