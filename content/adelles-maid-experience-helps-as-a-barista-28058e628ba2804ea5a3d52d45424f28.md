# Adelle’s maid experience helps as a barista

[Products: TWOLD](twold-e028aa0786f5449984a4f497c1d746fa.md)
[Scenes: Adelle becomes a barista](adelle-becomes-a-barista-21258e628ba280aca16ffd6e4fa4c8f1.md)

I want there to be some connection between [Adelle](adelle-2ff1980fd10b42a7bc0f65b61a2ab3e8.md)‘s jobs as a [maid](adelle-as-a-maid-e44bfe4304c44cfca11e22c29d2a08dd.md) and a [barista](adelle-as-a-barista-1d858e628ba280d9bac1cc43140ce0b9.md), where the former prepares her for the latter.

This should involve both earlier scenes calling out her preparing tea for nobles, and the particular ways that they want their tea, and the ways she is expected to make tea in the [Tea shop](tea-shop-27e58e628ba2801795a5c6553b61bb15.md).
