# The Huntsman

I want to make a monster hunter, but with a basis on actual, medieval hunting.

A hunter of animals that *sometimes* hunts monsters.

# Waidgerechtigkeit

From Google AI:

Waidgerechtigkeit" is a German term that refers to a hunting ethic, encompassing both written 
and unwritten rules, mastery of hunting skills, and a mindful approach 
to the hunt. It emphasizes minimizing pain and stress for the hunted animal and adhering to animal protection laws.

[According to Ami im Schwabenland](http://bhejl.blogspot.com/2019/10/hunting-in-germany-waidgerechtigkeit.html), it's a concept that goes beyond mere rules, encompassing a hunter's attitude and respect for nature.

Here's a more detailed breakdown:

- **Ethical Conduct:**
    
    Waidgerechtigkeit is a code of conduct for hunters, dictating how they should behave in the field.
    
- **Mastery of Skills:**
    
    It requires hunters to be proficient in their craft, ensuring they can make ethical and clean shots.
    
- **Respect for Game:**
    
    A core tenet is minimizing suffering for the hunted animal, reflected in the choice of weapons, hunting methods, and shot placement.
    
- **Environmental Awareness:**
    
    Waidgerechtigkeit extends to respecting the natural environment and adhering to all relevant laws and regulations.
    
- **No Direct English Translation:**
    
    While "fair chase" or "good huntsmanship" may come close, Ami im Schwabenland says there isn't a single English word that fully captures the depth and nuance of Waidgerechtigkeit.
