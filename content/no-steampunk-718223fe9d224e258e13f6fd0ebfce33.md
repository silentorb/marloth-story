# No steampunk

The title of this requirement is a little hyperbolic for effect.  There can be *some* steampunk elements in Marloth, I just want to keep them minimal and isolated to sub-areas.

By isolated, I mean steampunk can’t appear just anywhere, such elements need to appear either in designated locations, designated chapters, or wielded by designated characters.

Also, I want to avoid the steampunk aesthetic.  By nature, my style is subtle.  Steampunk, like other forms of punk, usually involve dramatic embellishments that are designed to draw attention, often negative attention.
