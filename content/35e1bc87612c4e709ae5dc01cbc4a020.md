# Millamer and Nivana

# Thinking of marriage

For a long time the soft plan has been to have [Millamer](../../Data/Characters/Millamer%20b9feb3f85e784020a074f460918e85b7.md) and [Nivana](../../Data/Characters/Nivana%20a2ee9cd0a28b4945af74fd3022fd97af.md) eventually marry.

They are a good “odd couple” with both interesting chemistry and interesting history.

# Speak now, or forever hold your peace

However, I don’t like the idea of a prominent Marloth marriage with two Ts.  Marloth is a romantic setting, and within the context of Marloth, I don’t find any prominent marriage between two Ts very interesting to write.

There’s plenty of room for double-T marriages in the background and supporting roles.

# Overlap

However, there are a lot of similarities between their storylines, so it would be good to at least unify their societal struggles if not their marital status.

Initially, Nivana looks down on Millamer, and Millamer resents Nivana.
