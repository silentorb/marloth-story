# Paprika Opening

> 💡 This was the original inspiration for the [Free spirited / adaptable main characters](../../Features/Free%20spirited%20adaptable%20main%20characters%201d458e628ba2803d9f73cd30fd59b6ef.md) motivation

Both visually and musically, the opening credits of Paprika is one of the most inspiring pieces of media I have ever witnessed.

The narrative intro preceding it also notably achieves Wonderland.

I don’t like the rest of the movie as much, and some of it is simply repulsive.

Unlike most narratives of this style, *Paprika* is unusually candid from the start, making it clear that teasing would be a consistent theme throughout the movie and any beauty or happiness in the movie will be only a tease with no fulfillment.

*Paprika* delivers exactly what it claims it will.
