# Family Wonderland


There is an inherent conflict between family and [Wonderland](wonderland-3cbc40d2ba2a4c76b4b9dc370452fcfe.md).

Family needs stability and routine.  [Wonderland](wonderland-3cbc40d2ba2a4c76b4b9dc370452fcfe.md) is essentially unstable and mercurial.

Family is based on commitment.  [Wonderland](wonderland-3cbc40d2ba2a4c76b4b9dc370452fcfe.md) is based on freedom.

# Free agents vs. Commitment

## Father/husband responsibilities

It is the responsibility of a husband/father to:

1. Provide for his wife and children
2. Protect his family
3. Lead his family
4. Be involved in the lives of his family

## Mother/wife responsibilities

It is the responsibility of a mother/wife to:

1. Support her husband
2. Raise the children
3. Manage the home

<aside>
💡

All father responsibilities for dependents falls on the mother if the father dies.

</aside>

# Family provision

Fathers/husbands must provide for their families.

Unless a father/husband is independently wealthy, he needs to work in order to earn that provision.

Providing for a family takes a significant amount of a father/husband’s time.

# Stability

An important aspect of family provision is a stable environment for the children to grow up in.

<aside>
💡

I have never heard people speak fondly of having to regularly move as a child.

</aside>

## Community

Part of the reason stability is important for children is “It takes a village to raise a child.”

Children need a good community to fully flourish.

It takes time for connections to be established between a family and a community.

The connections are a part of a family’s roots.

The connections need to be re-established any time the family is uprooted.
