# My own worst enemy

Make it clear that the misfortune’s Nivana wrestles with are largely her own doing.  The world is difficult but it is not out to get her.

> 💡 Despite all of this, Nivana still needs to be a sympathetic character.  She makes poor decisions while pursuing good things.
