# Build houses

> Build houses and live in them; plant gardens and eat their produce. Take wives and have sons and daughters; take wives for your sons, and give your daughters in marriage, that they may bear sons and daughters; multiply there, and do not decrease. - Jeremiah 29: 5-6

# Context

The children of Israel were sent exiled from their homeland due to their sin.  They planned on immediately returning, but Jeremiah told them they would not return for 70 years.

Jeremiah told them to settle down and invest in the land they are living in.

# Application

For Marloth, I want cases where characters are simply trying to survive and escape an unpleasant location, but where the heroes settle down and make it a home from which both themselves and others can benefit.
