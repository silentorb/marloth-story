# Wages of irresponsibility

When people—particularly people in positions of power—are irresponsible and their irresponsibility eventually catches up with them.

The consequences are usually spectacular.

# Time scale

This idea is not about immediate consequences.  This is not about someone making a mistake that bears fruit the same day.

This idea is about *lifestyle* choices.  About choices that become a reoccurring part of a person’s life.  The decision to make a perpetual compromise.

# Counter-point

The main purpose for this idea is to act as a foil for [Responsibility](../Articles/Responsibility%2023358e628ba280ca9e79ebeaa0fa931b.md).
