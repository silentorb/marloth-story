The orderly was sitting at the front of the room by the [desk window in the wall through which customers are assisted], reading a magazine about women.

Millamer and Fugue were sitting at a small table, playing cards.

Adelle was surprised to see Fugue.  “Fugue!  I didn’t know you worked for the agency.”

Fugue looked up from his cards.  “Who, me?  I don’t!  I would never work for [the bear].  I have more trust for Jakob Damond than that bear.”

“I’m not sure *I* follow,” said Adelle.

“We help people in need,” said Millamer, “we just can’t help *everyone*.”
