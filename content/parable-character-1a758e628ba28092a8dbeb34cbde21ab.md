# Parable character

A character who is used as an object lesson involving moral decisions.

Parable characters are usually tragic and demonstrate what a person *shouldn’t* do.

<aside>
💡

Parable characters are simpler than [Drama character](drama-character-1a758e628ba28093bb2ce20c597c8638.md)s, while their souls and motivations are explored more than [Elemental character](elemental-character-1a758e628ba2806bba7ec053001f2dd0.md)s.

</aside>
