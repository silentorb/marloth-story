# Conflicting accounts

Cases where different characters make different claims or propose different theories, and the story never explicitly states which one is true.

In some cases, the story can *imply* which account is true.

In other cases the truth is never implied.  In such cases, the audience tends to subconsciously create an amalgamated idea.
