# The Wizards’ Council

[Parents: Magicians](magicians-993b14e4e8e54ed8b690f56cea8429a2.md)
[Characters: Jarvanis](jarvanis-373b77c5f599467d9f1dbdd198cca4e5.md)

The largest organization of wizards in Marloth.  For a long time they have held a near monopoly on formalized magic practice.

They survive by tightly controlling knowledge.  They rely on esoteric convolution, tradition, and pride.

They are openly hostile to anyone or anything that threatens their control of magic.

# Beyond the council

There are darker aspects of magic that the Wizards’ Council has no control over.

Two of their primary competitors are [The Necromancers](the-necromancers-5fe18fead3844cea92136ea65ede0940.md), and [The Black Market](the-black-market-2fd545de1f944dcba025524099ced961.md).
