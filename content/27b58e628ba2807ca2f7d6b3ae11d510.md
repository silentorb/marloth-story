# Books are magical

This was a mechanic in [A Child’s Fairytale World](../Data/Products/A%20Child%E2%80%99s%20Fairytale%20World%20c368a02eabb74a47ab322615f2769723.md), and it may be worth including in more of the narrative.

# Problem

This is an extreme feature which makes high demands of its surroundings and can quickly create plot conflicts.

> 💡 It sort of worked in [A Child’s Fairytale World](../Data/Products/A%20Child%E2%80%99s%20Fairytale%20World%20c368a02eabb74a47ab322615f2769723.md) due to there being hardly any books in the world outside of [The Great Library](../Data/Locations/The%20Great%20Library%2027e58e628ba280b0948bd833ae53039b.md).

In order to continue implementing this idea, it would need one or more limiting mechanics to make its effects manageable.

# Limiting effect

## Only certain books

One limit would be only certain books or types of books are magical.
