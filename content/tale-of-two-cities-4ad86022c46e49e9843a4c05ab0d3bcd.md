# Tale of two cities

[Children: The Asylum spanning two cities](the-asylum-spanning-two-cities-2aa58e628ba280e6b826de187769f3e3.md)
[Products: A Child’s Fairytale World](a-childs-fairytale-world-c368a02eabb74a47ab322615f2769723.md)[, TWOLD](twold-e028aa0786f5449984a4f497c1d746fa.md)

Through much of TWOLD’s design process, I’ve envisioned having two cities, that are actually two angles (and maybe different stages) of the same city.

The two cities are [The Nameless City](the-nameless-city-2639e47762d1483c9c8619c59575b713.md) and [The City of Orphans](the-city-of-orphans-79bb690186294ddfbd417bfcc108b5ac.md).

It’s generally been a non-essential idea, but it seems easy to fit into most of the other elements and is useful.

Biblically, it seems like a great way to subtly overlay some of the prophetic themes of Israel, where the Nameless City is 2 Samuel era, and the City of Orphans is Ezekiel era.
