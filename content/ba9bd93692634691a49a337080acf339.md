# Nivana sorceress arc

# Overview

The main purpose of this arc is to depict a woman wrestling with her station as a woman, chafing at the leadership of men, and ultimately finding peace and fulfillment through embracing her natural role as a woman.

# Sister support arc

The original motivation for Nivana turning to magic was in desperation to support her bed-ridden sister.

At one point I discarded that idea but now it is viable again and is more attractive than before.

[Nivana supporting her sister](../../Solutions/Nivana%20supporting%20her%20sister%202aa58e628ba2801bbbdaef7a7af37013.md) 

# Flow

This arc has the following stages:

1. Opposed to magic
2. Embracing evil magic
3. Burned by evil magic
4. Embracing good magic
