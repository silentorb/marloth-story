# Wonderland

The eponym for [Wonderland](../../Articles/Wonderland%203cbc40d2ba2a4c76b4b9dc370452fcfe.md) .

It’s important to note that the essence of Wonderland I am looking for is partially lost in the last third of the story.

I consider the first part the strongest part of the book.  The middle part is chalk full of great content but it’s the first part that has the really impressive flow and constantly exquisite detail

> 💡 Marloth is heavily influence by Alice in Wonderland, but Marloth is an “Alice in Wonderland” variant.
> I want to capture the spirit of the story without the particular world elements.
